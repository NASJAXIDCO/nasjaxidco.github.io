Office Overview

We are the DEERS/RAPIDS ID Card Office located at NAS Jacksonville. Our mission is to deliver timely and accurate CAC and USID services to active duty, reservists, retirees, dependents, and DoD civilians.

Hours of Operation

Monday – Friday: 0730 – 1600
Closed on federal holidays.

Required Documents

Bring two forms of valid ID, one must be a state or federal photo ID. Additional documents may be needed depending on the service requested.
